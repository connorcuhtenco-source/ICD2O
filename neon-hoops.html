MOVEMENT
Use W A S D to move your player around the court.
Your player always dribbles automatically — watch the ball bounce!
💡 Press C to switch dribbling hands at any time.
🎯
SHOOTING
Hold LMB (left mouse button) to charge your shot. A meter will appear — release when the needle lands in the GREEN zone! The green zone is randomised every shot, so stay sharp.
💡 The green zone is wider when you're close to the hoop.
🏀
DUNKING
Get close to the hoop (within 3 units) and press SPACE to jump, then press SPACE again mid-air for a DUNK!
💡 Dunks score 2 points and never miss!
🤖
AI DEFENDER
Walk into the glowing purple zone near the far end of the court to spawn an AI defender. Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard!
💡 If the AI steals the ball, press F near them to block their shot!
👕
CUSTOMIZATION
Press ESC at any time during the game to open the customization menu. Change your skin, hair, outfit, hat, shoes, and face!
💡 Your choices are saved automatically.
